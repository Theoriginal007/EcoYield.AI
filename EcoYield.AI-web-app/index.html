Features

Our app brings cutting-edge machine learning to vertical farming, providing crop recommendations based on factors like light exposure, humidity, temperature control, water usage, nutrient levels, and weather conditions, all optimized for urban setups. Additionally, its deep learning-powered plant disease detection ensures fast, accurate identification of crop ailments, helping farmers protect their yields and boost productivity. This smart solution is a game-changer for sustainable and efficient vertical farming. It's a game-changer for agriculture!
